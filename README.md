Creature Drive

This repository is the collection of codes, asset, .apk, etc of my work of a 3D multiplayer fighting game with AR.
I named the game "Creature Drive".
In this game, we can choose 1 out of 2 characters which are all monsters.
We will use the monster to battle with another player in a room/stage.

Every character has 2 bars, HP and MP.
The winner is the first to deplete the other player's character's HP by attacking.

There are 3 kinds of attacks, they are normal attack, special attack, and super attack.
Initial MP value is 0 and will fill up if the character is attacked or its attack hits the opponent.
Special attack uses one-third of MP bar and super attack uses a full bar.
